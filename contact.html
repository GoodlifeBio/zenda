Work with us. Talk to us.

We are a small, focused team building something that has not existed before. If you are a researcher, institution, or fellow builder interested in what VRI could mean for your work, we want to hear from you.

For researchers — research collaboration

We are interested in partnerships with academic institutions, domain scientists, and research-forward organisations. VRI is designed to augment your team, not replace it. The concept paper is available upon request.

For builders — technical collaboration

VRI is being developed as an open architecture. If you work on multi-agent systems, scientific AI, agentic frameworks, or research orchestration, we'd like to talk.
