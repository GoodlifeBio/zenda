What we build.

Autonomous Agent Pipelines

Multi-agent systems that decompose complex scientific questions into tractable sub-problems, execute literature review, generate testable hypotheses, and self-validate through adversarial cross-checking.

Structured Reasoning Engines

Custom inference frameworks that enforce formal scientific reasoning — ensuring hypotheses include falsifiable predictions, control conditions, and quantified confidence intervals.

Human-AI Collaboration Layer

Interfaces designed for researchers to review, annotate, and redirect AI-generated research. Every interaction feeds back into the system, continuously improving hypothesis quality.

Knowledge Graph Architecture

A living, evolving scientific knowledge graph that tracks relationships between hypotheses, experimental results, contradictions, and emerging patterns across all active research domains.
