Virtual Research Institute

Autonomous research and discovery at scale.

New Horizon Labs is building VRI, Virtual Research Institute: a multi-agent AI platform for autonomous research across complex, knowledge-intensive domains.

The institute orchestrator manages a fleet of autonomous laboratories, each staffed with specialised AI scientists and other agents that review literature, generate hypotheses, design experiments, and synthesise findings across vast bodies of published knowledge. Not one AI assistant. Not one lab. An entire institute that generates laboratories on demand, staffs them with specialised AI scientists, and coordinates cross-lab research autonomously. Human researchers stay on-loop throughout. The discoveries compound continuously.

Starting in the life sciences. Expanding wherever structured empirical reasoning applies.

01 Access Inequality

Building a multidisciplinary team is a luxury. Under-resourced researchers cannot afford the risk of exploring creative, unproven ideas. So, they don’t.

02 Literature Overload

The volume of published research has outpaced any individual’s ability to synthesize it. Discoveries are being missed because nobody has looked. And it’s growing faster, faster than ever.

03 Slow Iteration

From hypothesis to validation to peer review, the cycle is measured in months or years. VRI compresses this into a continuous, autonomous Human assisted loop.
